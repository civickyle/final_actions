Committee Papers vs Personal Papers

Understanding Paper Types

Committee Papers: Legislation introduced by one or more committees (e.g., Finance Committee, Zoning Committee). These may represent items that originated from committee work, but they are typically items prepared by the administration.

Personal Papers: Legislation introduced by one or more councilmembers. These represent individual councilmember initiatives but are sometimes also prepared by the administration.
